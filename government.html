Regulated imports and exports

In our city we are mostly self sufficient, and therefore don't need to rely on massive imports or exports.

Population

Our city has a sustainable population size between 100,000 and 500,000 citizens.

Government-owned agriculture

The government owns 90% of produce from farmers to supply to state owned supermarkets or distribute to the citizens. The rest of the 10% is up to farmer discretion--they could keep it, sell it at farmers markets, restaurants, etc.
