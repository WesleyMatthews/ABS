Biofuel

Around the world food waste contributes to about 8% of greenhouse gas emissions. To combat this, we turn food waste into biofuel which provides energy for transportation. This process happens by essentially pressure cooking food waste until it is turned into a bio-oil which can then be refined into biofuel. At the moment, this process is not completely carbon neutral but it has less of a carbon footprint than food waste does.
